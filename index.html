Our Mission

We are an environmentalist group local to Co. Kerry, Ireland

We strive to preserve our coastal environment, waterways and marine life, while educating about pollution.

We also deeply value our local communities and through our events we hope to make new friendships and build valuable connections.

Clean Coasts

We clean local beaches every first and third Saturday of the month.

Make a Difference

It is rewarding to be part of a cause that cleans our environment from plastic pollution.

Make Friends

It is a great way to meet new people, engage with the local community and improve our environment.

Enjoy the Nature

While taking part in our events, enjoy the beautiful scenery of Co. Kerry coasts

Coffe Meetups

We host coffee and tea get togethers at the local cafes after every beach clean-ups

Surf Together

On the last Saturday of the month we organise community surf out - it is a lot of fun!

Why should you join Coast Keepers today?

Beach cleaning has been shown to cultivate a positive mood and feeling of fulfillment. Scientific studies found that that when beach cleaning, people reported a significant difference in the sense of meaning compared to walking on the beach or other beach activities

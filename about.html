Our Story

We are a group of friends that have been living and surfing together on Ballybunion beach for over ten years. In 2022, after a long break from socialising, we met up for our first surf after the covid pandemic. We have noticed a lot of plastic debris washing up at the beach; especially surgical masks and plastic capsules. We have always been passionate about our environments and the beaches, thus we came up with the Coast Keepers initiative; to encourage people to start engaging with local community again while cleaning up our beautiful North Kerry beaches and learning about the harm of coastal pollution.
